Welcome to Maze Runner Fan Page

Maze Runner is a thrilling movie series based on the books by James Dashner. It is filled with adventure, mystery, and suspense.

The series starts with a group of teenagers waking up in a mysterious maze with no memory of how they got there. Their survival depends on teamwork and solving the secrets of the maze.
